FAQ

Common Questions

Can I use more than one platform in my product range?

Yes. Many Liposoma clients use LiproteQ™ for capsule SKUs and LipoFlow for a liquid or shot product within the same range. Each platform is ordered and supplied independently. Contact us if you want to discuss a multi-format strategy.

What is the difference between LiproteQ™ and standard liposomal powder ingredients?

LiproteQ™ is produced via Liposoma's proprietary lipid micro-dispersion and spray-drying process. The platform is designed for manufacturing consistency, ingredient stability, and production line compatibility. LiproteQ™ consists of real liposomes with measurable particle size (DLS) and zeta potential.

Liposoma does not publish comparative claims against third-party products, but full technical documentation is available under NDA for qualified buyers to assess independently.

Do you supply consumer products or only bulk ingredients?

Liposoma supplies bulk B2B ingredients via LiproteQ™ and LipoFlow, and offers white-label and private-label products via both platforms. NutraTeq™ products are delivered filled and finished across multiple applications, including sachets, jars, and dispenser formats, ready for consumers or hospitality services. Liposoma does not sell direct-to-consumer products through its own brands.

Is NutraTeq™ the same as a liposomal product?

NutraTeq™ uses a patented lipid paste matrix rather than traditional liposomal encapsulation. It is a lipid-based delivery technology, but it is distinct from LiproteQ™ liposomes and LipoFlow liquid liposomes. It is designed as a food supplement format with multi-applicability across food-style formats, including functional toppings and dispenser-based applications in hospitality, hotels, sports, and wellness environments. The system features a high phospholipid content designed to support enhanced bioavailability.

Are your products available in sunflower lecithin?

Yes, our products are available in both sunflower and soybean lecithin.

Do you offer white label products?

In addition to private label custom development, Liposoma offers white label products for each of its platform technologies.
