Certifications & Standards

Built on Verified Quality Systems

Our manufacturing operates under internationally recognised quality frameworks that are standard requirements for distributors, supplement manufacturers, and regulatory submissions across EU, UK, US, and Australian markets. These are not aspirational frameworks — they are the active basis of how we run production in GMP certified supplement manufacturer environments.

FSSC 22000 / ISO 22000

Our food safety management system operates in line with FSSC 22000 and ISO 22000 standards — the internationally recognised benchmarks for food and supplement manufacturing. This gives our B2B partners confidence that production processes are structured, auditable, and consistent with global food safety expectations.

GMP-Aligned Manufacturing

Our manufacturing processes are aligned with Good Manufacturing Practice principles across formulation, production, and quality control. GMP alignment means controlled environments, documented procedures, and verification at each production stage — the baseline expectation for any serious nutraceutical manufacturing supplier.

HACCP-Based Risk Control

Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points methodology is applied across our production process. This means potential risks to product safety and quality are identified, assessed, and controlled at the points that matter — giving partners a structured risk framework they can reference in their own regulatory documentation.

Religious & Market Compliance

Halal-Certified Production Available

Liposoma holds Halal certification for two of its three manufacturing platforms — LiproteQ™ dry liposomal powders and NutraTeq™ lipid paste. Halal certificates are issued per applicable production batch and can be provided as part of standard documentation on request.

For partners supplying into markets in the Middle East, Southeast Asia, or any region where Halal compliance is a procurement or regulatory requirement, we can confirm certification status and documentation availability ahead of order placement.

Halal Certification Scope

Halal certification currently applies to LiproteQ™ dry powder and NutraTeq™ paste production batches. Contact us to confirm certification status for your specific active and order requirements before committing.

In-House Testing

We Test What We Produce

Quality claims without testing data are meaningless in this industry. Our in-house analytical team runs validation testing across all production batches, with results available in documentation form for partner review. We do not make formulation or performance claims we cannot support with data.

Physical & Structural Testing

  • Particle size analysis by Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS) — confirms liposome size distribution per batch
  • Encapsulation efficiency testing — verifies the proportion of active ingredient successfully encapsulated within the liposomal structure
  • Visual and physical inspection across production stages

Chemical & Stability Testing

  • Active content analysis by HPLC — quantifies active ingredient concentration per batch against specification
  • Stability testing under defined storage conditions — confirms product integrity across the stated shelf life
  • Residual solvent and contaminant screening beyond standard CoA parameters where required

Data-First Manufacturing Philosophy

Liposoma was built on the principle that health product manufacturers should only sell what they can verify. Every batch we produce leaves our facility with testing data attached, not after-the-fact assurances. Our Scientific Advisors — including academic specialists in lipid-based drug delivery — bring more than 40 years of formulative expertise to how we design and validate every production process.

Documentation Per Batch

Every Batch Ships With Full Documentation

For distributors managing import compliance, manufacturers requiring ingredient verification, and regulatory teams preparing market authorisation files — documentation is not optional. We issue a complete documentation set per production batch as standard.

Documentation Types

📄

Certificate of Analysis

Full quantitative analysis of active ingredient content, purity, and physical characteristics per batch. Available for every production run as standard, with no minimum order threshold for documentation access.

🔬

Material Safety Data Sheet

Full safety and handling data per ingredient and format. Required for import, logistics, and internal handling documentation in most regulated markets.

📋

Product Specifications Sheet

Detailed technical specification per ingredient — including physical form, particle size range, encapsulation method, carrier composition, and storage requirements.

✅

Residual Solvent & Contaminant Declarations

Where required, we provide declarations covering residual solvents and contaminant screening results. These go beyond standard CoA parameters for partners operating in regulated pharmaceutical or clinical nutrition markets.

Need these files before procurement review? We can issue available documentation for your target market before order commitment.
